## W3W.delivery’s Privacy-First Approach

At W3W.delivery, protecting the privacy of drivers and customers is essential. By using TikTok for public confirmation and lead generation, we minimize data collection while safeguarding sensitive information through a secure, compliant framework.

### Key Privacy Compliance Measures

1. **Data Minimization**  
   We collect only the essential data needed for delivery and lead confirmation. TikTok videos serve as public confirmations, reducing additional data collection and keeping personal details private.

2. **Explicit Consent**  
   Before engaging in TikTok-based delivery confirmations, drivers and customers are fully informed and give consent, fostering trust while respecting privacy rights.

3. **Secure Content Handling**  
   All content shared on TikTok is securely managed. Sensitive data is not stored unless essential, with content designed to be shareable and free from private details.

4. **Anonymized Posting**  
   Drivers omit specific identifiers, such as names and locations, in TikTok posts, ensuring privacy while allowing for public engagement with our brand hashtags.

---

## Privacy Support and Alternative Options

For users preferring not to use TikTok, we offer alternative confirmation methods. Our support team is available to address any privacy concerns, providing solutions that maintain service quality.

### Commitment to Compliance and Transparency

W3W.delivery continuously updates privacy practices to comply with evolving regulations, offering a secure, transparent experience that respects data security for both drivers and customers.

This privacy-first approach enables W3W.delivery to maintain a trusted, safe environment for content-driven delivery confirmation, empowering users with control over their data and interactions.
