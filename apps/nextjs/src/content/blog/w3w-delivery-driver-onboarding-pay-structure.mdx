## W3W.delivery’s Flat Fee Model

Every standard delivery within a 10-mile radius of the pickup location is set at a flat rate of **$33 USD**, paid in Solana $SOL. This simple fee structure provides consistency for drivers, and it includes content capture, such as photos, video clips, or livestreams, which can be shared by both the customer and business for greater visibility.

For deliveries that extend beyond this range, customers have the option to increase the fee 2x or 3x, scaling from $33 to potentially **$99 USD**. This added cost compensates drivers for the additional distance and time while maximizing the reach and visibility of each delivery through engaging, driver-generated content.

## Content Creation and Quick Payments for Drivers

To thrive with W3W.delivery, drivers need a basic level of content creation experience. Familiarity with editing tools like TikTok and CapCut helps drivers produce short, engaging reels or duets for each delivery. With these tools, drivers can easily add company logos and follow clear content instructions, ensuring each video is shareable, engaging, and consistent.

Upon posting the required content as a TikTok duet or reel using W3W.delivery’s specified hashtags, drivers receive instant payments directly to their Solana Phantom wallets. **Payouts are typically completed within 1-8 hours**, enabling quick earnings after every successful delivery.

## 1-2-3 Onboarding: Donate Your First Delivery

1️⃣ **Submit Verification**: Confirm your active driver status on another platform (e.g., DoorDash, Uber Eats) to fast-track the application process.

2️⃣ **Donate Your First Delivery**: Show commitment by donating your first delivery fee to a designated charity through W3W.delivery. This one-time donation builds community trust and sets you up as a dedicated driver within the platform.

3️⃣ **Deliv3ry ID Effect**: Once your first delivery test is completed, order a branded Deliv3ry driver effect used to verify each delivery on TikTok with a #deliv3ry hashtag that can include a duet or livestream depending on the customers instructions.
